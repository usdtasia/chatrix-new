Live Chat for E-Commerce: Reduce Cart Abandonment and Boost Sales · Prochat

How live chat helps e-commerce businesses in Uzbekistan reduce cart abandonment, answer product questions, and increase order value.

E-commerce stores lose 70% of potential buyers at checkout. Most of them had a question that wasn't answered in time. Live chat for e-commerce solves this at the moment it matters most.

Where Chat Impacts E-Commerce Most

  • Product pages: "Is this in stock?", "What size should I order?"
  • Shopping cart: Last-minute doubts, promo code questions
  • Checkout: Payment method help (Uzcard, Humo), address confirmation
  • Post-purchase: Delivery tracking, returns

Reducing Cart Abandonment

Set a chat trigger for visitors who've been in the cart for 30+ seconds without checking out:

"Having trouble checking out? I can help with payment options including Uzcard and Humo."

This single trigger typically recovers 5–10% of abandoned carts.

Using AI for Product Questions

Train your AI on your product catalog: dimensions, materials, compatibility, availability. Customers get instant answers at 11 PM when your operators are offline. They don't leave to find an answer — they buy.

📊 A Tashkent electronics retailer reduced cart abandonment from 74% to 58% in 6 weeks after deploying Prochat's AI on product and cart pages.

Upselling and Cross-Selling via Chat

When a customer asks about a product, your AI can naturally suggest complementary items: "This laptop is popular — many customers also add the carrying case. Want me to check availability?"

Local Payment Support

Uzbek customers frequently ask about Uzcard and Humo payment options, installment plans, and bank transfers. Pre-load your AI with clear answers to these — they're conversion blockers if unanswered.
